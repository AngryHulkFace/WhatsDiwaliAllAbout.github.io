This is a page on Diwali(why and how we celebrate it)





why Diwali is celebrated
Diwali celebrates Rama's victorious return to his homeland after defeating the evil spirit Ravana. Because the celebration coincides with the Hindu New Year, the business community believes it an auspicious time to start new companies.


how Diwali is celebrated
People dress up in their best clothes for Diwali, decorate their homes with diyas and rangoli, perform worship ceremonies for Lakshmi, the goddess of prosperity and wealth, light fireworks, and share mithai (sweets) and gifts.
